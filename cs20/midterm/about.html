About Us

Our Story


Hain's Bakery was founded in 2020 by four friends: Helena, Alex, Isaiah and Noah. What started as a fun hobby in one of our home kitchens has grown into much more than just a business, but rather a community where we strive to bring joy to our customers with our fresh pastries and welcoming shop, all while supporting our local farmers by sourcing our ingredients close to home.
